About Wheat Auto Works

We have been providing industry-leading automotive solutions around the Harrisburg, NC area for many years.

From 2017-2021, Thomas served as a 2146 Main Battle Tank Repairer in the United States Marine Corps. The MBT repairer/technician performs basic automotive duties incident to inspection, maintenance, and repair of the Marine Corps MBT, Tank Retriever (M88A2), and Armored Vehicle Launched Bridge (AVLB). The MBT repairer/technician also completes basic shop administrative forms and records utilizing technical manuals.

In 2022, Thomas started at University of North Carolina at Charlotte for Business to support his passion for automotive excellence and to grow his company.
