Website Evaluations

Parts Geek

Poor: Parts Geek is a website for car parts.

  • Contrast - Very basic on colors, nothing really pops out, white background
  • Repetition - The menu items and parts as you scroll down seem to be the only things consistent. Other parts are different sizes and fonts.
  • Alignment - The entire site is centered and very cluttered. Horizontally, most things are aligned after the big mess in the front page, which doesnt look good at all
  • Proximity - Everything is bunched up together and has no gaps or dead space.

Youtube

Good: YouTube is a video and music sharing website and application.

  • Contrast - Offers a light and dark mode for user preference, which can also help during low light. Buttons and titles are easily seen.
  • Repetition - Everything is where you would expect it to be and nothing stands out in a bad way. Most buttons are the same size, font and color.
  • Alignment -The alignment is generally clean and functional. Key elements like the sidebar, video suggestions, and main content area are well-aligned, providing a structured layout.
  • Proximity - The sidebar with shortcuts to subscriptions, trending videos, and library sections is placed close together for quick access.
